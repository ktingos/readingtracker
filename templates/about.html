About Reading Tracker

Reading Tracker is a website designed to help you keep track of your reading. It makes it easy for you to log the books that you have read and keep track of when you read them. It also allows you to log the reading that you do every day so that you can look back and see how many pages you read on any given day.

This was created for the CS50 final project at Harvard College. I chose this idea because I have always loved reading but have not done it nearly as much in recent years. By making it so easy to keep track of my reading, my hope was to motivate myself and others to continue reading as much as possible.
